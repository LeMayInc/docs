LeMay Commerce billing is handled entirely through Shopify's native billing system. Charges appear on your regular Shopify invoice alongside your Shopify subscription and other app charges.

## Subscription Tiers

| Tier | Monthly | Tools | Target |
|------|---------|-------|--------|
| Startup | $29 | 65 | Solo operators and new stores |
| Small Business | $99 | 80 | Growing stores with expanding operations |
| Business | $149 | 105 | Established brands with multi-channel needs |
| Corporate | $249 | 129 | Full-capability operations |
| Enterprise | $499 | 129 + Automation | High-volume stores requiring automated workflows |
| LeMay Agency | $14,999 | 129 + Dedicated AM | White-glove managed commerce operations |

## Upgrading

Navigate to **Shopify Admin** > **Apps** > **LeMay Commerce** > **Settings** > **Subscription**. Select your target tier and confirm. Shopify will process the upgrade immediately, and the additional tools become available within seconds.

Upgrades are prorated for the remaining billing period.

## Downgrading

Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Tools beyond your new tier's allocation will become unavailable at that time. No data is lost during a downgrade — tool configurations and history remain intact.

## Cancellation

Cancel your subscription through the standard Shopify app removal flow: **Shopify Admin** > **Apps** > **LeMay Commerce** > **Remove app**. This immediately revokes the access token and deactivates all tools. You will be billed through the end of your current billing period.

## LeMay Agency Tier

The Agency tier is not a software subscription. It is a managed service engagement. A dedicated LeMay account manager operates your commerce tools on your behalf, executing workflows, generating reports, and managing day-to-day operations.

Agency tier inquiries: [agency@lemay.app](mailto:agency@lemay.app)
