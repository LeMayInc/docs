title: "Products"

---

The Products category covers the full product catalog lifecycle — creating new products, managing variants and options, publishing to sales channels, and organizing with collections and tags.

## Available Tools

| Tool | Description | Tier |
|------|-------------|------|
| `products_list` | List products with filters for status, type, vendor, and collection | Startup |
| `products_search` | Search products by title, SKU, barcode, or tag | Startup |
| `products_get` | Retrieve a single product with all variants and media | Startup |
| `products_create` | Create a new product with variants, pricing, and media | Small Business |
| `products_update` | Update product details, pricing, or metadata | Small Business |
| `products_delete` | Remove a product from the catalog | Business |
| `products_variants` | List and manage variants for a product | Startup |
| `products_images` | Manage product images and media | Small Business |
| `products_collections` | Add or remove products from collections | Small Business |
| `products_publish` | Publish or unpublish products to sales channels | Business |
| `products_count` | Count products matching specified criteria | Startup |
| `products_bulk_update` | Update multiple products in a single operation | Corporate |

## Example Commands

```
Show me all draft products
```
Routes to `products_list` with status filter `DRAFT`.

```
Create a new product called "Heritage Wool Scarf" at $89.00
```
Routes to `products_create` with title and price parameters.

```
Update the price of SKU-WS-001 to $94.00
```
Routes to `products_update` targeting the specific variant by SKU.

```
Publish all products in the "Winter Collection" to the online store
```
Routes to `products_publish` with collection and channel filters.
