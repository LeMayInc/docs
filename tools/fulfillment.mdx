---
title: "Fulfillment"
description: "Ship orders, generate labels, track packages, and process returns."
---

The Fulfillment category manages the post-purchase logistics pipeline — from creating shipments and adding tracking numbers to processing returns and managing third-party logistics providers.

## Available Tools

| Tool | Description | Tier |
|------|-------------|------|
| `fulfillment_create` | Create a fulfillment with line items and tracking | Small Business |
| `fulfillment_update` | Update tracking information on an existing fulfillment | Small Business |
| `fulfillment_cancel` | Cancel a fulfillment (unreceived by carrier) | Business |
| `fulfillment_list` | List fulfillments for an order | Startup |
| `fulfillment_tracking` | Add or update tracking numbers and carrier information | Small Business |
| `fulfillment_services` | List configured fulfillment services | Business |
| `fulfillment_orders_list` | List fulfillment orders with routing details | Business |
| `fulfillment_orders_move` | Move fulfillment orders between locations | Corporate |

## Example Commands

```
Ship order #1042 with USPS tracking 9400111899223456789012
```
Routes to `fulfillment_create` with carrier and tracking number.

```
Update tracking on order #1038 to UPS 1Z999AA10123456784
```
Routes to `fulfillment_tracking` with updated carrier and number.

```
Show me all unfulfilled fulfillment orders assigned to the Boston warehouse
```
Routes to `fulfillment_orders_list` with location and status filters.
