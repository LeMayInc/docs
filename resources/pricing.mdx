## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I switch plans at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately with prorated billing. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

**Is there a free trial?**
Contact [sales@lemay.app](mailto:sales@lemay.app) for trial access consideration.

**Do I need a specific Shopify plan?**
LeMay Commerce works with any Shopify plan — Basic, Shopify, Advanced, or Plus.

**What payment methods are accepted?**
Billing runs through Shopify. Whatever payment method is on your Shopify account applies to LeMay Commerce charges.
