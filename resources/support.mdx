## Getting Help

When contacting support, include the following:

1. Your Shopify store URL (e.g., `your-store.myshopify.com`)
2. Your LeMay Commerce subscription tier
3. The specific tool or command that produced the issue
4. The exact error message or unexpected behavior observed
5. The AI client you are using (Claude Desktop, Cursor, etc.)

This information allows our team to reproduce and resolve your issue without unnecessary back-and-forth.

## LeMay Agency Support

Agency tier subscribers receive dedicated support through their assigned account manager. Contact your account manager directly or reach the Agency support desk at [agency@lemay.app](mailto:agency@lemay.app). Agency support operates with a four-hour response target.
